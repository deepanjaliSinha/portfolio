Disney+Hotstar

Improving Hotstar App Design

(Group Project | Merge Magic Completion by XLRI | UI/UX Designing)

About the Competition

TeXLence'22 is the Annual and largest Techno Product Management festival across Indian B-Schools, organized by Prometheus, XLRI's Product, Technology, and UI/UX Committee. The fest aims to fraternize with industry stalwarts and enthusiasts by tagging 2022 with stellar CXO summits, gripping case competitions, live projects, research problems, and many more fun-filled events. Merge Magic is an online competition held by the committee and is open to all students irrespective of college.

Expectations

In the second round, we were expected to choose an anchor app. Based on the choice of app, we need to identify pain points or problems associated with that app currently. Post which, instead of trying to design feature which can help solve those problems, we need to merge another mobile application (associated app) which might already have those core features which could help solve the problems that we have identified.

Anchor and Associate App

Anchor App- Disney+ Hotstar has about 61.3 million of total users with breakup of users as Disney+ users: 38.7 million and Hotstar users: 22.6 million. Disney+ Hotstar has the highest amount of app downloads in the OTT industry

Associated App- IMDb has around 250 million unique monthly visitors and its audience segmentation in different age group range as, 25-34 year old: 29.75% and 18-24 year old: 24.78%. This age group division is significantly important for Hotstar, looking at the customer segmentation it cover. We chose IMDb to rectify the design errors or rather improve the existing design.

Team Details

  • Team Name: Mind Crusaders
  • Group of 2 people

Pain Points

  • Lack of personalized recommendations.
  • No Ratings displayed for movies/shows etc. (including parental ratings).

Solutions

Associated app will be used in:

  • Providing personalized recommendations based on genres
  • Add ratings as well as parental guides to each movie/show on anchor app.
  • Add user and critic reviews about the movie/show.

Project Duration

7th Dec - 16th Dec, 2022
